Alambre

Alambre is a popular Mexican dish consisting of grilled beef topped with chopped bacon, bell peppers, onions, cheese, salsa and, in some variations, avocado.

Beef ball

Beef ball is a commonly cooked food in Canton and overseas Chinese communities which was originated by Teochew people.

Bistek

Beef sirloin or tenderloin, salt and pepper. Regional variations may include onions, garlic, oregano, cumin, calamansi juice, soy sauce.

Carne

Carne asada is a dish of grilled and sliced beef, usually skirt steak, sirloin steak, tenderloin steak, or rib steak.

Galbi

Galbi is a type of gui (grilled dish) in Korean cuisine. Galbi is the Korean word for rib and the dish is usually made with beef short ribs.

Mitsado

Mitsado (or mechado) is a braised beef dish originating from the Philippines inspired by culinary methods of Spain, which it was a former colony of.
